Our Services

Lomo Link delivers skills acquisition programme, professional training, and training on IELTS. We have a local presence in some of the UK’s biggest cities, with campuses in London, Birmingham and Manchester.

Consultancy Services

Our bespoke service is specially crafted to fit your unique needs. Some of our types of services are listed below:

  • Advising Overseas organisations looking to bring skilled talent to the UK
  • UK students don’t want to go through the UCAS process
  • Researching the top colleges in the UK
  • Preparation for interviews
  • Understanding Oxford and Cambridge
  • Confidence coaching

We love the fact that we have strong relationships with Universities. What makes it sweet is that we provide regular qualified applicants from multicultural backgrounds. The smartest Universities champion a diversity of thought.

We believe that we are enriching the pipeline - and we are only able to do that because of the outreach activities that we do and the fact we are curious about understanding the needs of International students. We are fortunate enough to be able to visit Universities several times a year to learn about the differences in education systems and more about student life. This helps us improve our service to Students.
